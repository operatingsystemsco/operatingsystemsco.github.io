Corrigenda

The Open Group publications deal with technology at the leading edge of open systems development. Occasionally errors or inconsistencies are uncovered during implementation and demand a change to the published document. Only significant changes are affected. These are now reported by means of individual corrigenda referenced to a specific specification or guide. The corrigenda can be viewed at The Open Group's web site ( http://www.opengroup.org/corrigenda/).

The corrigenda published to date affecting the Single UNIX Specification, Version 2 are included on this CD-ROM:
