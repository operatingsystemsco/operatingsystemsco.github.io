CORRIGENDA:

ABSTRACT:The Open Group publications deal with technology at the leading edge of open systems development. Occasionally errors or inconsistencies are uncovered during implementation and demand a change to the published document. Only significant changes are affected. These are now reported by means of individual corrigenda referenced to a specific specification or guide and are listed below. The corrigenda can be viewed at The Open Group's web site (http:\\www.opengroup.org\public\pubs). To see the latest index by email, send a message to ftpmail@xopen.org containing the following four lines in the body of the message: open; cd pub/Corrigenda; get index; quit. Use the same email address to request a copy of the full corrigendum information following the email instructions.
